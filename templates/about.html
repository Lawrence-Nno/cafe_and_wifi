About Us

At Cafe and Wifi Spaces, we are committed to providing you with a detailed list of all Cafes and work friendly spaces all over cities and their detailed features, so you know what to expect ahead of time.
If any of the Cafes are obsolete or closed please feel free to contact us, so we can issue you a "delete key" after confirmation to use in the deletion of the said cafe from our database with the methods provided. You can even add new cafes and spaces we might have missed or edit already listed cafes with any changes that might have occurred.
We have built this site like a community service where anyone can easily make contributions for the public to benefit from.
Help share the word and the link. Thanks!.
